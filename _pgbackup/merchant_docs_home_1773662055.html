Everything you need to set up, configure, and manage your MPay Merchant Wallet.

Overview

The MPay Merchant Wallet is a powerful mobile payment acceptance solution designed for businesses of all sizes. Accept payments seamlessly through QR codes, NFC tap-to-pay, and direct wallet transfers — all from your smartphone.

With the Merchant Wallet, you can manage your transactions, generate detailed reports, configure payment preferences, and provide your customers with a fast, secure checkout experience.

  • Accept QR code payments instantly
  • NFC tap-to-pay contactless payments
  • Real-time transaction monitoring
  • Detailed sales reports & analytics
  • Multi-currency support
  • Bank-grade security & encryption
  • Customizable business profile
  • Export transactions (CSV, PDF)
New to MPay? Start with our Getting Started guide to download the app and set up your first merchant account in minutes.
