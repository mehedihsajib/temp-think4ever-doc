Wallet Management

Learn how to check your balance, add funds, withdraw money, and understand your transaction limits.

Your MPay Wallet

Your mPay wallet is a secure digital wallet that stores your funds and allows you to make payments, transfer money, and manage your finances — all from your smartphone.

Checking Your Balance

Your wallet balance is always visible on the home screen of the mPay app. Here's everything you need to know about viewing and understanding your balance.

How to View Your Balance

1

Open the MPay App

Launch the mPay Consumer app on your device. Your balance appears prominently on the home screen.

2

View Available Balance

Your available balance is displayed at the top of the screen. This is the amount you can spend or transfer immediately.

3

Toggle Balance Visibility

Tap the eye icon () to hide or show your balance for privacy when in public.

Pending transactions may temporarily reduce your available balance. Pending amounts are shown separately below your main balance.

Top-Up Methods

Add funds to your MPay wallet using any of the following methods. Each method has different processing times and fees.

Bank Transfer

Transfer funds from any local banks to your mPay wallet account via Instant Payment processing. Safe and fast.

Instant

Wallet to Wallet

Instantly load your wallet account by asking another mPay customer to send you money and pay them cash.

Instant

Cash Agent

Visit an authorized mPay merchant near you to deposit cash into your wallet. Present your mPay QR code to the merchant.

Instant

Withdrawals

Withdraw funds from your wallet to your linked bank account. MPay supports instant withdrawals to major local banks.

Important: mPay adheres to Curaçao Central Bank policies. Ensure bank account details are accurate and correct prior the transaction to avoid any delay.
